Are you a parent seeking to actively participate in your child's learning journey?


Look no further. Try our dedicated platform for enhancing your child's education while fostering a stronger parent-child bond.


TouchVision, our mission is to empower visually impaired children with innovative and engaging tools for learning Braille. We believe that every child, regardless of their abilities, deserves access to quality education. With this belief at our core, we have developed a user-friendly web application that employs state-of-the-art Convolutional Neural Networks to recognize Braille characters from user-captured images.

Our mission is to bridge the gap in Braille education and support visually impaired students:

  • Fostering motivation through engaging Braille materials.
  • Empowering parents with Braille knowledge to enhance children's learning.
  • Addressing the unique challenges of remote learning for visually impaired students during the pandemic, by providing essential tools and school support.
